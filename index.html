General Info

This website was made solely for students of IIT Madras to make informed decisions about their courses and professors.Whether you're choosing your next elective or evaluating a professor, this website allows students to:

  • Explore detailed information on courses offered at the college.
  • Read and share reviews and feedback from fellow students about courses and professors.
  • Utilize the CGPA predictor, powered by machine learning algorithms, to estimate your future CGPA based on your performance in previous courses.
